description

The **Soonly Bootstrap Template** is a clean, modern, and responsive "coming soon" template ideal for pre-launch websites. Featuring a minimalist design, it includes an animated countdown timer, a customizable progress bar, and social media links to keep users engaged while the main site is under development. Perfect for businesses, startups, and portfolios, Soonly offers an elegant way to maintain a professional online presence and gather leads before the official launch.

layout

The **Soonly Bootstrap Template** layout is structured to offer a sleek and focused "coming soon" page, organized for clarity and user engagement:

1. **Hero Section**: The main, full-screen hero section prominently displays a background image or video, with an overlay that maintains readability. Here, the site's title or logo is centered, paired with a concise tagline or launch message.

2. **Countdown Timer**: Below the hero, a countdown timer dynamically displays the days, hours, minutes, and seconds until launch. This is often styled with large, clear typography to draw attention.

3. **Subscription Form**: Positioned either directly beneath the timer or alongside it, the email subscription form invites visitors to sign up for updates. This form is simple, with fields for emailand a submit button, ensuring easy access.

5. **Social Media Links**: Social icons are usually placed in a row near the bottom of the page or as a fixed sidebar. This enables visitors to follow social profiles and stay updated

6. **Footer**: The footer contains essential links like privacy policy or contact information, styled minimally to keep the focus on the launch message

The layout is fully responsive, ensuring that each element adapts well on both desktop and mobile views. The minimalistic style and structured sections maintain a professional, engaging feel throughout.
